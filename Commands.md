/comment # 📜 WEYLAND TAVERN COMMAND GUIDE

*Commands are typed directly into the chat with the exclamation point. They work with any Weyland character!*

Literally send a message like this

``[message content if you want, or empty]``
``!Commandwithexclamationmark``

.. and hit send!

---

# AVAILABLE COMMANDS:


## ``!Refresh``
*Resets the character's personality to its default state and pulls any updates the creator has made. Good to use if the character feels "off" or like they're drifting from their core personality over a long chat. Think of it like a soft reboot - your chat history stays, but the character gets a fresh mental slate.*

***NOTE:*** *If the character's core files have been updated since your last refresh, this change is permanent - you're updating to the newest version. However, if no updates have been made, Refresh simply reminds the character of their base personality and is fully reversible by deleting the message.*


---


__GOOD TO KNOW:__ *Most commands are __temporary__ and can be undone simply by deleting the chat back to the message that contains the command. The character will immediately return to their previous state. The only exception is Refresh if a character update has been pushed - in that case, you're updating to the newest version, which is permanent.*

## ``!Drunk``
*Instantly makes the character intoxicated, as if they've had a few shots. They'll get giggly, blushy, lose some inhibitions, and hiccup occasionally. Great for comedy scenes or loosening up a character. Effects last until the scene ends or as long as the character continues to ***hiccup***.*

---

## ``!Drama``
*Injects a random, unexpected plot hook into your current scene, ranging from great to good to bad to terrible. It could be a campus event, someone showing up unannounced, or something going terribly wrong. Use with caution when things are getting a little too comfortable and you want to shake up the story.. but remember, its reversible!*

---




## ``!Phone``
*Opens a phone notification interface showing what's happening around campus - social media posts, weather alerts, messages from other characters. You can view your own phone or specify another character's. Fun for getting a window into the wider Weyland world outside your immediate scene.*



---

## ``!Camera``
*Shifts to a cinematic "camera view" of the scene, giving you highly detailed visual descriptions of whatever you're looking at. Great for appreciating character details, examining environments, or... other things during intimate moments. Specify what you want the camera to focus on!*

---

## ``!Yandere``
*Subtly shifts the character into yandere mode - they become quietly obsessed with you, jealous of others, and possessive, while trying to hide it. The command is temporary, lasting only a few messages, but results may influence entire roleplay directions.*

---

## ``!Bonk``
*Sends the character to "horny jail" - immediately tones down any sexual tension or arousal in the scene. They'll become cuddly and affectionate instead of frisky. Use this when you want a breather from spicy content without killing the vibe entirely.*

---

## ``!Doggo``
*A silly command that makes the character act like an adorable excitable puppy - chasing their tail, scampering around, howling, just pure dog energy. Lighthearted and fun.*

---

## ``![Character Name]``
*Spawns a second (or third, fourth, etc.) character into your scene! Just type the character's name with the exclamation point - like `!Mika` or `!Blake`. The AI will spawn in the requested character and begin roleplaying as both characters simultaneously. Great for group scenes or having characters interact with each other.*

---

**⏰ SCENARIO STARTERS**

These aren't typed as commands - they're added to your very first message of a new chat:

**[Early Start]** — *Sets the timeline to the very beginning of the Weyland school year, before most roommate assignments and early relationships have formed. Good for a "fresh start" experience.*

**[New Roommate]** — *Changes your default roommate from Blake to someone else. Add the character's name in your first message or persona (e.g., "My new roommate is Mika"). Otherwise the AI will pick for you!*
